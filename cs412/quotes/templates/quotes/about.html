About

Vincent van Gogh was born on the 30th of March 1853, in the Netherlands. He was a Post-Impressionist painter whose paintings became famous and influential following his death. Over his life, he painted more than 860 oil paintings, which can be found in museums all over the world. He suffered from psychotic episodes and spent time in psychiatric episodes. On the 29th of July, 1890, he committed suicide at the age of 37.
